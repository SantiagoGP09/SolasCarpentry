Hardwood Flooring

Floors that feel as
good as they look.

A great floor changes everything about a room — the warmth, the acoustics, the way light moves through the space. Noe works with hardwood, engineered wood, laminate, and luxury vinyl to give you the look and durability that fits your life.

Every installation starts with a proper subfloor inspection and moisture assessment. No shortcuts, no squeaks six months later.

  • Hardwood installation — solid and engineered options
  • Laminate and luxury vinyl plank (LVP) installation
  • Hardwood refinishing — sanding, staining, sealing
  • Damaged board repair and replacement
  • Subfloor leveling and preparation
  • Stair treads and transitions between rooms

Doors & Windows

First impressions
start at the door.

A poorly fitted door rattles, sticks, and lets in drafts. A beautifully installed door is silent, solid, and tells guests everything about the quality inside. Noe treats every door and window installation with the same precision he brings to fine furniture.

Whether it's a single interior door or a full exterior replacement with new framing, the fit is always perfect and the finish is always clean.

  • Interior door installation and replacement
  • Exterior entry doors — pre-hung and custom frames
  • French doors and sliding barn doors
  • Window trim, casing, and sill installation
  • Door and window frame repair or rebuilding
  • Weatherstripping and seal work for energy efficiency

Drywall & Finishing

Walls so smooth,
you'll forget they were fixed.

Great drywall work is invisible — and that's exactly the point. Whether it's a fresh installation for a new room, a patch after water damage, or a complete texture refresh, Noe delivers results that blend seamlessly with the rest of your walls.

He's done it all: smooth finish, orange peel, knockdown, skip trowel. The technique is always matched to what already exists in your home.

  • New drywall installation — walls and ceilings
  • Patch and repair — holes, cracks, water damage
  • Texture matching — orange peel, knockdown, smooth
  • Popcorn ceiling removal and re-texturing
  • Corner bead installation and taping
  • Ready-to-paint finish — sanded, primed, and perfect
